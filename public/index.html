What is Grouproom?

Videochat doesn't work as well for large groups or social situations. By letting participants move around themselves, Grouproom gives individuals their freedom back. Three people on one side of the room can be having a conversation completly separate from a group of four on the other side.

Collaborate on work or socialize, totally free for up to 10 participants. No time limits + end-to-end encryption on every meeting.
